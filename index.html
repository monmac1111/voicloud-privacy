Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Voicloud ("the App"), developed by Mon Software, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Data We Collect

The App may access the following data, all of which remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to upload it:

  • Audio Recordings — Voice recordings you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Location Data (Optional) — If you grant permission, the App may tag recordings with your location. This data is stored locally and is never sent to our servers.
  • Calendar Data (Read-Only) — If you grant permission, the App reads your calendar events to suggest file names for recordings. The App does not modify or store your calendar data.

2. Cloud Uploads

You may choose to upload recordings to your own cloud storage accounts, including Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, and iCloud. These uploads are initiated solely by you and go directly to your personal accounts. We do not have access to your cloud storage credentials or uploaded files.

3. No Developer Servers

Voicloud does not transmit any of your personal data, recordings, or transcriptions to servers owned or operated by the developer. All processing happens on your device or within cloud services you have explicitly authorized.

4. Transcription

Speech-to-text transcription is performed entirely on your device using on-device engines (WhisperKit, Apple SpeechAnalyzer). No audio data is sent to external servers for transcription purposes.

5. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain device and usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. For details on how Google handles your data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

6. Analytics

We do not collect any analytics data. No usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral data are sent to the developer.

7. Children's Privacy

Voicloud is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Voicloud no transmite ninguno de sus datos personales, grabaciones o transcripciones a servidores propiedad del desarrollador o gestionados por este. Todo el procesamiento se realiza en su dispositivo o dentro de los servicios en la nube que usted haya autorizado explícitamente.

4. Transcripción

La transcripción de voz a texto se realiza íntegramente en su dispositivo utilizando motores locales (WhisperKit, Apple SpeechAnalyzer). No se envían datos de audio a servidores externos con fines de transcripción.

No recopilamos ningún dato analítico. No se envían estadísticas de uso, informes de errores ni datos de comportamiento al desarrollador.

Voicloud no está dirigida a menores de 13 años. No recopilamos deliberadamente información personal de menores de 13 años. Si cree que un menor nos ha proporcionado datos personales, póngase en contacto con nosotros para que podamos tomar las medidas oportunas.

8. Cambios en esta política

Podemos actualizar esta Política de Privacidad de vez en cuando. Los cambios se reflejarán en esta página con una fecha de vigencia actualizada. Le recomendamos revisar esta política periódicamente.
