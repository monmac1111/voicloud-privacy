Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

This policy explains how the iOS app "Voicloud Direct" (the "App"), developed by Mon Software, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and processing data transparently and with minimal collection.

1. Data We Access

The App accesses the following data. All of it is stored locally on your iPhone and is never sent anywhere unless you explicitly choose to upload or transmit it.

  • Audio recordings — Audio recorded via the microphone is stored locally on your iPhone.
  • Transcripts & summaries — Text generated or retrieved by the App is stored in a local store (SwiftData) on your device.
  • Location (optional) — Captured only if you turn on the feature that stores the recording location as metadata. It is saved on-device alongside the recording and is never sent to the developer. You can turn it off at any time.

2. OS Permissions Used

  • Microphone — Required to record audio.
  • Location (optional) — Used only to store the recording location as metadata.
  • Calendar (optional) — Used only if you enable automatic file naming from calendar events. Event information is used solely to generate the file name on-device and is never transmitted externally.

3. Cloud Upload (optional)

You may upload recordings to your own iCloud Drive / Google Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive / Box account. These uploads go directly to your own account and do not pass through any developer server. OAuth credentials and tokens are stored only in your device's Keychain.

4. AI Features (Transcription & Summarization / BYOK)

Transcription and summarization use a "BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)" model. They are optional features and require you to configure an API key you obtained yourself from an AI provider. Supported providers are OpenAI, Google Gemini (Google AI Studio), and Google Vertex AI.

Audio or text is transmitted only when you have configured an API key and run transcription or summarization. In that case, the selected recording's audio or its text is sent directly from your device to the API of the provider you chose (OpenAI or Google), authenticated with your own API key. Voicloud Direct does not relay your data through any server of ours. Billing and data handling at the destination provider are governed by that provider's terms and privacy policy.

If you do not configure an API key, no audio or text is ever sent to any AI service. Core features — recording, playback, and exporting via the share sheet — work without any key.

API keys are stored encrypted in your device's Keychain. The developer cannot access them, and they are never sent to any developer server.

5. No Transmission to Developer Servers

Voicloud Direct never sends your audio, transcripts, summaries, or API keys to any server owned or operated by the developer (we do not operate any AI inference server). All processing happens on your device, within cloud services you have explicitly connected, or — for AI features only — with the AI service you explicitly invoked after configuring your own API key (opt-in).

6. Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics SDK (Google Analytics, Firebase, Sentry, etc.). We do not collect behavioral data or usage statistics, and we do not perform any tracking.

7. Advertising

The App does not display ads. It is a paid app and contains no advertising SDK.

8. Children's Privacy

Voicloud Direct is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
